How it works

From sign-up to results in five steps

You build it, we approve it, your people take it, and you get a benchmarked report — with Shiftwork Solutions guiding the way.

1

Request a demo & get set up

Tell us about your operation — industry, size, departments, and current schedule. We set up your company workspace with a secure login.

2

Prepare your survey

Pick from a 30-year question bank, choose the real schedules for employees to rate, and select the breakouts you want your results sliced by — age, department, job title, shift, and more.

3

Get it approved Shiftwork Solutions

We review your survey to make sure the benchmark comparisons stay valid and the questions fit your operation. Once we greenlight it, you're clear to launch.

4

Administer the survey

Employees respond anonymously through a number-gated link — no accounts, no names, just honest answers from the floor.

5

Close it & get your results

When you're ready, close the survey and download a benchmarked PDF report — broken out exactly the ways you chose, with recommendations ranked by safety and cost impact.
